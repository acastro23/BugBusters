Woods Inn

Tucked away in the serene embrace of a lush forest, Woods Inn offers an idyllic escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. This cozy cabin-style hotel invites you to reconnect with nature in a tranquil setting where the only sounds you will hear are the rustling of leaves and the songs of birds. Designed for peace-seekers and adventurers alike, the retreat is the perfect place to recharge and embrace the simplicity of the outdoors. Guests can choose between two room options: a Single Bed Suite for solo travelers, and a Double Bed Suite, ideal for families or friends looking to share their getaway. Both styles combine rustic charm with modern comfort, offering premium bedding, large windows to soak in the forest views, and amenities designed to make your stay unforgettable. Whether you spend your time unwinding on a private porch, exploring nearby hiking trails, or simply enjoying the stillness of the woods, Woods Inn promises an experience of rejuvenation and harmony with nature.
